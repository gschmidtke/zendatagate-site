What ZEN does
One gateway, three core capabilities.
Guid-driven Gateway
ACC & Revit, finally in one story.

ZEN reads from your ACC registries, link matrices, and model lists, then opens the right cloud models with a GUID-driven engine. No more manual “which RVT is where?” detective work.

Batch Automation
Health checks & exports while you sleep.

Schedule model health checks, sheet and schedule exports, and family analytics across dozens of models. ZEN runs the batch, writes reports, and leaves your federated project quietly updated each morning.

Data Discipline
Registries as the source of truth.

Drive automation from structured registries — sheets, links, worksets, families. ZEN treats Excel and the CDE as first-class citizens so project data stays auditable, reviewable, and ready for BI dashboards.
